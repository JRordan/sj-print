How it works

From file
to finished.

Three steps, Two hours, No surprises. Upload your art, approve a digital proof, and have your order in hand. We handle the rest.

— 01

Upload your design

Drop in a PDF, AI, PSD, or PNG. Not press-ready? Our prepress team will flag bleed, color, and resolution issues before anything hits the press.

— 02

Approve a proof

A free digital proof within four business hours. Need a physical proof on your final stock? We do those too — typically same-day pickup.

— 03

Pickup or delivery

Same-day local pickup at our San Jose shop, free Bay Area delivery on orders over $250, or carbon-neutral nationwide shipping.
